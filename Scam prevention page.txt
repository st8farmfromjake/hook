How can I tell a real email from a fake one? 
Here are a few ways to tell the difference between fake and real emails: 


* Check for spelling,  grammatical or other errors such as,
   * Using the wrong form of their
   * Missing or over use of punctuations
   * Spelling errors in general
   * Check for different fonts, text sizes, or colors


* Make sure that the email address is trustworthy,
   * Check for obviously fake or scam email addresses “thisisfake@jsjs.org”
   * Make sure its someone from the company, but also not an impersonator
   * Check for any misspellings in the address as well


* Don't click on ANY links from untrustworthy senders
   * Look into the link directly before clicking on the links
